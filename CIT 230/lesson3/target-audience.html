Target Audience

Audience description.

Personas

Persona 1:

    George Smith

  • Occupation: Retired Teacher
  • Demographics & Education: 65 years old, senior citizen, Master level education in Secondary Education.
  • Motivations for using this site: Learn the weather conditions for his favorite fishing areas.
  • Social: Loves being outside and spending time with friends and family.
  • Technology: Prefers to use his desktop computer.

Persona 2:

    Blake Johnson

  • Occupation: Manager at a local sports equipment store.
  • Demographics & Education: 25 years old, Bachelor level education in Business.
  • Motivations for using this site: Wants to know what is happening in his area for the weekend.
  • Social: Spends a lot of time with friends, going to different events, and being outdoors.
  • Technology: Mostly uses his iPhone, does a lot of work on his laptop.

Scenarios

  1. What can I expect in this area today?
  2. Where would be the best place to spend the weekend?
  3. Are there any local events I should go to?
  4. Is it worth spending some time outside of my house today?
  5. I'd like to know more information about a neighboring town.
