Reading Notes

Saving/Using Storage in JavaScript:

  • use: localStorage.setItem(key, value)
  • retreive: var x = localStorage.getItem(key)

JSON:

  • Converts anything to text and returns it to its original type.
  • JSON.parse(variable) - converts a variable back to its original type by 'parsing'.
  • JSON.stringify(variable) - converts a variable to a string.

Clearing Content

  • document.getElementById('id').innerHTML = null - can clear content ex: previous output, placeholders, etc.
