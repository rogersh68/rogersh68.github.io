Site Purpose

The Black Cloud Weather website's purpose is to provide detailed weather information for South East Idaho. The site is meant to help the user plan for the weather in their area and understand what to expect. These objectives will be achieved with descriptive articles regarding the events in each town and a forecast for the weather in that area.
